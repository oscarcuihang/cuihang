A persone who loves Nature and It's creations

Hang Cui was born on July 6th, 1990 in Zhengzhou, China, the capital of Henan Province. He now lives in Columbia, Missouri, USA and studies at the University of Missouri-Columbia in Computer Science and Information Technology majors.

At the age of 15, Hang got his first digital camera from his dad. Since that time, he was the photographer in his class. After starting high school in 2006, Hang joined the Robot team in his high school, Henan Experimental High School. As the team leader, he led his team and won lots of competitions during his 3 years of high school. Because of these robots, he started coding and loved to code. At the beginning of 2011, Hang came to the US and started his university life at MIZZOU. Of course he is still travelling around the country and taking photos!

Summer 2014, I was a software developer intern in Vagabond Vending Inc. This company is trying to bring everything in the database to visual data. Customers could manager their vending machines through our software. They can monitor the real-time sales, and amount of those sales. The previous data for sales were coming from USA-technology. But they are developing hardware to ignore using data from other database. Which mains, in the future, every individual sale that happens under our hardware will be directly sends to our database. And customer could see them on time. What I did for them this summer is I re-built the Internal admin Tools, which is a major part for the company. This tool allows admins to see pretty much everything in our customer information database and manage these data. The tool also saves lots of time for our CEO, managers, and even myself to update anything might happen.
